Implementation details:

The game is essentially just a lattice gas simulation on a 4-neighbor square grid.
The underlying reaction-diffusion model is the cyclic competitive Lotka-Volterra model,
with a bit of diffusion-limited aggregation thrown in.

For some more cool ecological cellular automata, check out the NANIA example applets:
http://www2.ph.ed.ac.uk/nania/examples.html
